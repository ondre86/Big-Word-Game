How to Play
Type a word into the box and press "enter". You gain a point for each syllable detected in the word.

Rules
  • Play in alphabetical order
  • Play within ten seconds (10 sec.)
  • At least three syllables long (3)
  • Not offensive
  • No reused words
Break a rule, get a strike.

Three strikes and you're out!
