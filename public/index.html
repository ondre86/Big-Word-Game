The Big Word Game is simple.

You have ten seconds to enter a three-syllable word in alphabetical order to gain points.

You get a point for each syllable detected in the word.

If your submission is not a word, not the correct starting letter, too short, or offensive, you receive a strike.

Three strikes and you're out!
