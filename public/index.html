How to Play
You have ten seconds to type and enter a word that starts with the correct letter and contains at least three syllables.

You gain a point for each syllable detected in the word.
examples:
an * gry 2 syllables = 0 points  ❌
 as * tro * naut3 syllables = 3 points  ✅
Rules
  • Word must contain at least three (3) syllables
  • Words must be played in the correct order
    (A to Z, and back to A)
  • Play within 10 seconds
  • No offensive words
  • No reused words
Break a rule; get a strike.

 Three strikes and you're out!
