How to Play
You have ten seconds.

Type a word that:
  • starts with the current letter
  • AND
  • contains at least 3 syllables
You gain a point for each syllable detected in a correct word.
examples:
an * gry 2 syllables = 0 points  ❌
 as * tro * naut3 syllables = 3 points  ✅
 as * tro * lo * gy4 syllables = 4 points  ✅
Rules
  • Play within 10 seconds
    (timer stops when input is out of view or game is paused)
  • Word must contain at least three (3) syllables
  • Word must start with correct letter
  • No offensive words
  • No reused words

Break a rule; get a strike.

 Three strikes and you're out!

not 100% accurate, so have fun! :)
