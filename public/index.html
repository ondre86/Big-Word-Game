How to Play
Enter a word that starts with the target letter and contains at least 3 syllables.

You gain points for each syllable detected in a correct word.

The target letter advances after each correct word played.

Play until you strike out.
examples:
an * gry 2 syllables = 0 points  ❌
 as * tro * naut3 syllables = 3 points  ✅
 bal * le * ri * na4 syllables = 4 points  ✅
Rules
  • Play within 10 seconds (you can pause with the button or by scrolling past the input)
  • Word must contain at least three (3) syllables
  • Word must start with the correct letter
  • No offensive words
  • No two-part words (no hyphens or spaces)
  • Cannot reuse words
Break a rule; get a strike. Three strikes and you're out!

War Mode Play the biggest word!
Two players battle to enter the word with the most syllables that starts with the target letter.

The highest syllable word wins.
If there is a tie, players repeat the same letter until one word is higher.

The target letter advances after a word has won.

You gain points for each syllable detected in the winning word.
